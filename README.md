# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis & Retention Strategy

## 🔍 Project Overview
This project analyzes customer churn patterns to identify high-risk segments and evaluate the financial impact of retention strategies using ROI-based scenarios.

## 🧠 Key Objectives
- Identify at-risk customers
- Analyze churn by region, connection type, and customer value
- Estimate financial benefits of retention programs
- Support business decision-making using data

## 📈 Key Insights
- **30% of customers are at risk of churn**
- **Wireline customers show significantly higher churn than wireless**
- **Low-value (<1M) customers contribute the highest churn**
- Retention programs show **ROI up to 5900%** across scenarios

## 🗺️ High-Risk Areas
Top churn-prone regions include:
- Andaman & Nicobar
- Himachal Pradesh
- Haryana
- Rajasthan

## 💰 Financial Impact
- Investment: $1.52B  
- Revenue Saved: $91.5B  
- Net Benefit: $89.9B  
- ROI: **5900%**
